The difference between margins, borders, and padding is in it’s positioning. Padding is closest to the content, it is the space between the content and the border. The margin is outside of the border of the content. It can be made visible and and encapsulate the content. The margin is outside the border. It is transparent. The margin represents the space between the content and other content.

So for example, you could have some text, “pigs,” the padding would represent the area between the word and the borders of the text box. The margins would then be how close that word falls in relation to other elements on the page, like other texts boxes or image.
